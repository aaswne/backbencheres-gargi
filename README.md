# Backbenchers Gargi

Budget backpacker stay & Kerala homely food in Gargi near Kasol.

Stay cheap. Eat homely. Wake up to snowy mountain views — from just ₹299.

---

## About

Backbenchers Gargi is a small startup mountain stay built inside an old village house in Gargi, Himachal Pradesh.

We created a simple backpacker-friendly space for travelers looking for:

- Budget stay near Kasol
- Kerala homely food
- Peaceful mountain vibes
- Shared bed spaces
- Local village experience

This is not a luxury hotel — it’s a cozy mountain stay for real travelers.

---

## Features

- Beds starting from ₹299
- Kerala-style home food
- Mountain & valley views
- Backpacker-friendly atmosphere
